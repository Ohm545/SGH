About Complaint Express

Complaint Express is an AI-powered platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and government officials. We streamline the complaint process, ensuring that every voice is heard and addressed by the right department. Our mission is to promote transparency, enhance accountability, and empower citizens to participate actively in governance.
